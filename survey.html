Missing Data (you can help fill it) vs. AAU Campus Climate Survey**

In 2015 and 2019, the Association of American Universities (AAU) and its 27 and 33 member schools respectively conducted a survey “to examine the prevalence of, and assess the campus climate regarding, sexual assault and misconduct at colleges and universities”.

Problem: General Approval vs Lived Experiences

The 66% reflects an approval rate among all interviewees, regardless if they have experienced campus assault or if they have filed a report with the institution. What are the actual experiences of those who have experience campus sexual harassment or sexual assault?

**The survey broke reports on sexual assault and reports on nonconsensual sexual contact by force or inability to consent as two different categories.
