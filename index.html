The Cost of School Should Not Include Sexual Violence*

In the States, 1 in 5 women and 1 in 16 men are sexually assaulted while in college.

Title IX, a federal civil rights law originally enacted in 1972 to provide equal academic and athletics opportunities for women and men, covers a braod scope of issues, and has been commonly mobilized in campus sexual misconduct cases.

In February 2020, Secretary of Education Betsy DeVos introduced new guidelines on the regulation of Title IX, rolling back many of the strict interpretations from the Obama administration.





*Slogan from protest organized by National Women's Law Center outside the Department of Education on July 13, 2017, demanding that DeVos uphold Title IX protections on college campuses.
